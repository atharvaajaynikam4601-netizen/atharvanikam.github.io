About Me

I am a Mechatronics Master’s student in Germany with a strong foundation in Mechanical Engineering and hands-on industry experience in CAD design, FEM analysis, and industrial project execution.

I have worked on special-purpose machines, hydraulic systems, tooling design, and manufacturing optimization projects, using tools such as CATIA V5, SolidWorks, and ANSYS. My work focuses on practical engineering solutions that combine design accuracy with real-world manufacturability.

Currently, I am specializing in electrical drives and power electronics, with a strong interest in automotive systems, electrified powertrains, and sustainable engineering technologies.
